Of Origami
Designs:

✦Creativity & Art– Transforms simple paper into artistic masterpieces.
✦Cultural Significance – Rooted in Japanese tradition, now practiced.
✦Variety of Styles – Includes animals, flowers, geometric shapes, and modular structures.
✦No Tools Needed – Only paper, folding techniques, and imagination.
✦Educational Value – Enhances focus, patience, problem-solving, and motor skills.
✦Decorative Purpose – Used in home décor, gifts, and event decorations.
✦Symbolism – Certain designs (like the crane) symbolize peace, luck, and hope.
✦DIY Craft – Easy to learn, suitable for all age groups.
✦herapeutic Effect– Promotes mindfulness, stress relief, and relaxation.
✦Eco-Friendly – Uses recyclable materials with no wastage.
