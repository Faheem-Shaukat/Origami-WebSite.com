Easy Step by Step Instructions: Lucky Star

Step One:

Start with a long, narrow strip of paper. The exact measurements are not important. Form a loop at one end of the strip.
Pull the loop through, leaving only a small strip of paper at the end.

Step Two:

Flatten the loop so that it forms a pentagonal shape.

Step Three:

Tuck the remaining small strip of paper under the two top layers of the loop.

Step Four:

Flip the model around.

Step Five:

Fold the pentagonal portion down, aligning the top left edge with the width of the long strip.

Step Six:

Fold down the pentagonal portion again, aligning the top right edge with the strip. Note that the edge being aligned is different from that of Step 5.

Step Seven:

Continue to repeat Steps 5 and 6, alternating the edges that are lined up. Stop when there is only a small strip remaining.

Step Eight:

Tuck the small remaining strip under the top two layers, similar to Step 3.

Step Nine:

And now, the fun part! Pinch at the corners to puff up the star.

Tadaa!! Your origami star is completed.

NOTE:If you are a beginner, then use Kami paper to make origami. While for experts, You can use Origamido paper, Tant paper or double tissue paper
