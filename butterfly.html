Easy Step by Step Instructions: Butterfly

Step One:

Start with a square sheet of paper with the white side up. Fold it in half horizontally, crease it well, and then unfold it.

Step Two:

Fold the paper in half vertically, crease it well, and then unfold it.

Step Three:

Fold the top quarter of the paper along the dotted line to the center line. Crease this fold well and then unfold it.

Step Four:

Fold the bottom quarter of the paper along the dotted line to the center line. Crease this fold well and then unfold it.

Step Five:

Fold the right quarter of the paper along the dotted line to the center line. Crease this fold well and then unfold it.

Step Six:

Fold the left quarter of the paper along the dotted line to the center line. Crease this fold well and then unfold it.

Step Seven:

Fold the paper in half diagonally. Crease it well and then unfold it.

Step Eight:

Fold the paper in half diagonally the other way. Crease it well and then unfold it.

Step Nine:

Turn the paper over so the colored side is facing up.

Step Ten:

Fold the corner of the paper to the center along the dotted line. Crease this fold well and then unfold it. Do the same for all the corners.

Step Eleven:

Turn the paper over so the white side is facing up again.

Step Twelve:

Fold the two sides of the paper towards the center along the dotted line. You already have a crease here. Only fold the middle half of the paper where the dotted lines are. Donâ€™t fold the top or bottom parts of the existing crease.
You should have a shape that resembles what you see on the second diagram. Fold both the top and bottom of the model along the dotted lines to the center.

Step Thirteen:

This is a completed Boat Base. Fold the bottom two flaps of paper down along the dotted lines.

Step Fourteen:

Flip the model over.

Step Fifteen:

Fold the top part of the paper down along the dotted line.

Step Sixteen:

Flip the model over again.

Step Seventeen:

Fold the top flap of paper on the right along the dotted line towards the center. The dotted line goes from the top corner to halfway down the diagonal line at the bottom, as you can see in the diagram.
Fold the top flap of paper on the left along the dotted line towards the center. The dotted line goes from the top corner to halfway down the diagonal line at the bottom, as you can see in the diagram.

Step Eighteen:

Flip the model over.

Step Nineteen:

Valley Fold along the dotted line in the direction of the arrow in the diagram. Crease this fold well and then unfold it.
Do the same on the other side in the direction of the arrow in the diagram.

This gives the butterfly its final shape.

Tadaa!! Your origami butterfly is completed.
