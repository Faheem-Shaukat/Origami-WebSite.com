Easy Step by Step Instructions: Heart

Step One:

Start with the white side of the paper facing up. Fold the paper in half vertically, then unfold it to create a center crease.

Step Two:

Fold the paper in half horizontally, then unfold again.

Step Three:

Fold the bottom edge up to meet the center crease.

Step Four:

Turn the paper over.

Step Five:

Fold the bottom edges diagonally upward so they meet the vertical center crease.

Step Six:

Turn the paper over.

Step Seven:

Fold the top edges diagonally downward to meet the vertical center crease.

Step Eight:

Fold the top flap down, leaving the white triangles on the sides unfolded.
Continue folding the flap down while gently lifting the white triangles to avoid creasing them.

Tip: To keep everything neat, hold the bottom layers in place with your fingers when folding the flap down.

Step Nine:

Fold the white triangles inward along the dotted lines.

Step Ten:

Fold all four top corners downward to meet the crease created in Step 8.

Step Eleven:

Fold the top two corners down to meet the edge created in Step 8.

Step Twelve:

Turn the paper over.

Tadaa!! Your origami heart is completed.

NOTE:If you are a beginner, then use Kami paper to make origami. While for experts, You can use Origamido paper, Tant paper or double tissue paper
